Battalion 1944 Roadmap

  • Q1 2018
    Early Access Release
    • ALL future DLC will be free

    • Spring Q1 Update: The Stability Update

      At release to Early Access, we will inevitably find bugs. The team will be focusing their efforts on fixing existing issues that the community find, making sure EVERYONE can play our existing content, before we move on to the new content.

      • Stability & Performance improvements, fixing stabilisation issues, making sure the game is playable for EVERYONE
      • Map In our first major update we will release a new map to both casual & competitive rotations
      • FeatureOffline LAN Support across all modes
      • EventTwitch AMA with Brammertron & KingHoward on the future of Battalion 1944
      • Regular Fixes & Patches
  • Q2 2018
    After Early Access

    • Summer Q2 Update: The Content Update

      Providing a successful Early Access Launch, we are excited to support our first official ‘major’ LAN tournament. At the same time we’ll be releasing new and exciting content designed to diversify and reinvigorate the Battalion gameplay experience.

      • EventFirst Major LAN Tournament: To be announced
      • Arcade Map New map focused on Capture the Flag and Domination
      • Competitive Map Announcing Soon
      • Weapon Two New Undisclosed Weapons
      • Regular Fixes & Patches
  • Q3 2018
    Autumn Update

    • Autumn Q3 Update: The Competition Update

      It’s time for us to get serious about esports. By Q3 we will have balanced the gamemode and correlated all of your feedback to create an excellent competitive game. Here’s what we plan to add and improve for the competitive experience

      • Event Second Official ‘Major' LAN Tournament: To be announced
      • Competitive Map Announcing Soon
      • Feature Spectator Mode Overhaul for Esports
      • Feature ‘Clanwars’ Launches – In-game clan system for scrims, ladders and competitions with high tick servers
      • Regular Bug Fixes & Patches
  • Q4 2018
    Winter Update

    • Winter Q4 Update: The Beginning

      After one year of playing, refining and improving Battalion’s intricacies with the community. We will be hitting Steam as a fully released title. At this point, the price of the game will increase. This final update to early access will not be the last update. It is the beginning of Battalion’s life as a full blown competitive game.

      • Feature Theatre Mode
      • Feature New HUD / UI
      • Casual Map Announcing Soon
      • General Fixes & Patches ready for full release!
